My Story

The Beginning

People have been eating cupcakes for 100 years, but it was in the 1990s when i first moved from Hongkong to Australia through the shared love of cooking with my dad that I became passionate about interested in baking and makeing sweet delights like cupcakes.

The Online Store

I started baking cupcakes at home and its recenly that through encouragment from my friends at work that I ventured into this adventure of opening my first online presence as a cupcake connoisseur.

Wendy's Cupcakes Store
