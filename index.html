My Story



My Story Beginning

People have been eating cupcakes for 100 years, but it was in the 1990s when i first moved from Hongkong to Australia through the shared love of cooking with my dad that I became passionate about interested in baking and making sweet delights like cupcakes.

The Online Store

I started baking cupcakes at home and its recently that through encouragement from my friends at work that I ventured into this adventure of opening my first online presence as a cupcake connoisseur.

Wendy's Cupcakes Store<

What I love about cupcakes is that they can be varied with beautiful decorations and different tastes. There isn't a right or wrong and what's most important is that they're tasty! My dream is to open my cupcake store and share my love and passion with cupcakes with people.
